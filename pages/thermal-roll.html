Thermal Paper Rolls

At Papyrex, we specialize in the manufacturing and supply of all kinds of thermal paper rolls, designed to meet the diverse requirements of businesses across multiple industries. From daily retail billing to large-scale industrial applications, our thermal rolls are engineered for reliability, clarity, and consistent performance.

We understand that every machine and business has unique printing needs. That’s why Papyrex offers a complete range of thermal rolls, available in various sizes, core types, coatings, and paper grades.

Complete Thermal Roll Solutions by Papyrex

We deal in every major category of thermal rolls, including but not limited to:

POS Thermal Rolls

Used in retail stores, supermarkets, restaurants, and billing counters for fast and clear receipt printing.

ATM Thermal Rolls

Designed for banks and financial institutions, ensuring smooth operation and precise transaction printouts.

Thermal Paper Rolls for Billing Machines

Compatible with billing systems, cash registers, and handheld printers used in shops and service centers.

Barcode Thermal Rolls

Ideal for barcode printing in logistics, warehousing, inventory management, and shipping operations.

Label Thermal Rolls

Used for product labeling, price tags, packaging, and courier labels with excellent print contrast.

Medical & Diagnostic Thermal Rolls

Suitable for ECG, ultrasound, and medical reporting machines where accuracy and readability are critical.

Weighing Scale Thermal Rolls

Commonly used in grocery stores, meat shops, and retail weighing machines.

Parking & Ticketing Thermal Rolls

Perfect for parking systems, toll booths, cinemas, and event ticketing machines.
